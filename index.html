About Me

Hi, I'm David — a 15-year-old self-taught hyperrealism artist based in Sligo, Ireland.

I've been drawing since the age of 5, developing my skills through dedication, patience, and constant practice without formal training. My work focuses on capturing extreme detail and realism, aiming to bring every subject to life through graphite.

I specialise in highly detailed Formula 1 drawings and custom pieces created from client photos. Each artwork is carefully crafted with precision and attention to detail.

Today, I create commissioned pieces and original artworks for people who want something personal, meaningful, and unique.
